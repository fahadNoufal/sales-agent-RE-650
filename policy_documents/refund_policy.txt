ROYAL ENFIELD BULLET 650 - REFUND AND BOOKING CANCELLATION POLICY

1. VEHICLE BOOKING REFUNDS:
- Pre-order/Booking Amount: If you have placed a formal booking for the Bullet 650 and wish to cancel before the invoice is generated, you are eligible for a refund.
- Processing Fee: A nominal cancellation charge (as per regional dealership norms, typically ₹500 - ₹1,000) may be deducted from the booking amount.
- Refund Timeline: Once the cancellation is confirmed, the balance will be credited to your original payment source within 7–10 working days.

2. TEST DRIVES:
- Test drives are complimentary. No charges are collected, and therefore, no refunds are applicable for test drive appointments.

3. ACCESSORIES & APPAREL:
- Return Window: Genuine Royal Enfield accessories or apparel purchased at the time of booking can be returned within 15 days of delivery, provided they are unused and in original packaging.
- Non-Refundable Items: Electrical components or customized/fitted parts are not eligible for return or refund once installed on the motorcycle.

4. NON-REFUNDABLE SCENARIOS:
- Statutory Charges: Registration fees, road tax, and insurance premiums paid to the government or third-party providers are non-refundable once the vehicle registration process has initiated.
- Post-Delivery: Once the vehicle is invoiced and delivered to the customer, it cannot be returned for a refund under standard operating procedures.

5. HOW TO CLAIM:
To initiate a refund for a vehicle booking, the user must visit the dealership where the booking was placed or submit a request via the Royal Enfield App using the Booking ID.

6. CONTACT:
For refund status updates, please email: support@royalenfield.com or provide your Booking ID to this assistant.
