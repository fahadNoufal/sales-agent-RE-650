ROYAL ENFIELD BULLET 650 - TEST DRIVE CANCELLATION & RESCHEDULING POLICY

1. CANCELLATION PROCESS:
Users can cancel their test drive appointment at any time through the chatbot or by calling the dealership directly. To cancel via the chatbot, simply type "Cancel my test drive."

2. RESCHEDULING:
We understand plans change. You can reschedule your ride to a different time slot or date at no extra cost, subject to motorcycle availability at your chosen dealership.

3. NO-SHOW POLICY:
If a user does not arrive within 30 minutes of their scheduled slot without prior notice, the slot will be released to other waiting riders.

4. ELIGIBILITY & DOCUMENTATION:
Please note that the test drive may be cancelled by the dealership on-site if:
- The rider fails to produce a valid Permanent Driving License (Motorcycle with Gear).
- The rider is not wearing appropriate footwear (closed-toe shoes are mandatory).
- The rider appears to be under the influence of alcohol or any intoxicating substance.

5. WEATHER & SAFETY:
Royal Enfield reserves the right to cancel or postpone test drives due to extreme weather conditions, mechanical maintenance, or safety concerns. In such cases, the user will be notified and offered an immediate reschedule.

6. CHARGES:
There are NO cancellation fees for test drive appointments.

7. CONTACT SUPPORT:
For urgent cancellations or queries, please contact the Royal Enfield Central Helpline at 1800-210-0008 (Toll-Free).
